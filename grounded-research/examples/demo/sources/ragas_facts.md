From Es et al. (RAGAS), arXiv:2309.15217, Sep 2023; EACL 2024 demo track:
RAGAS is a framework for reference-free evaluation of RAG pipelines. The
faithfulness metric breaks the generated answer into individual statements and
checks whether each can be inferred from the retrieved context. RAGAS reports
three dimensions: faithfulness, answer relevance, context relevance.
